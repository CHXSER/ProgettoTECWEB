About

BOHEMY is an art society from Antartica with a passion for creating art, bringing fictional, imaginary worlds to life.

Our art is inspired by the world around us, and we very much enjoy working with a variety of different mediums, including digital art, watercolor, and acrylics.

We are constantly exploring and discovering new thing about art, which makes us excited for the future as we continue to grow our own perspective and present interesting ideas to the world with a bohemian touch.

What is the bohemian style ?

The bohemian style is a vibrant and electric expression of creativity that embraces the unconventional. It is not bound by rules, often merging various art forms, cultures, and historical influences. It is characterized by rich patterns and vibrant colors while mixing the natural and surreal.

To some, it expresses a celebration of individuality, freedom, and the joy of creating without boundaries. To others, it is an expression of experimentation, exploration, and a personal worldview aesthetic.
